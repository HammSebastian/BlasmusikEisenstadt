Program Overview

Our Youth Group is the heart of Blasmusik's future. Established in 1995, this program has been instrumental in developing young musicians and fostering a love for brass band music in our community. We welcome students aged 8-18, regardless of their current skill level.

Under the guidance of experienced instructors and mentors from our main band, young musicians receive comprehensive training in both individual technique and ensemble playing. Our program emphasizes not just musical skills, but also teamwork, discipline, and the joy of making music together.

What We Offer

Individual Lessons

One-on-one instruction tailored to each student's needs and skill level, focusing on proper technique and musical development.

Ensemble Playing

Regular group rehearsals where students learn to play together, developing listening skills and musical teamwork.

Performance Opportunities

Regular concerts and competitions where students can showcase their progress and gain valuable performance experience.

Music Theory

Comprehensive music education including theory, sight-reading, and music history to build well-rounded musicians.
