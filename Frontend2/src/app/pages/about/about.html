Our Story

Founded in 1985 by a group of passionate musicians, Blasmusik has grown from a small community ensemble into one of the region's most respected brass bands. Our journey began with just eight members who shared a common love for brass band music and a vision to bring this beautiful art form to our community.

Over the years, we have performed at countless events, from intimate community gatherings to large-scale festivals and competitions. Our repertoire spans traditional brass band classics, contemporary arrangements, and original compositions that showcase the versatility and power of brass instruments.

Today, Blasmusik consists of 25 dedicated musicians who come together regularly to rehearse, perform, and share their passion for music with audiences of all ages. We are proud to be an integral part of our community's cultural landscape.

Our Mission

Musical Excellence

We strive to maintain the highest standards of musical performance while making brass band music accessible and enjoyable for all.

Community Engagement

We believe in the power of music to bring people together and enrich our community through regular performances and educational outreach.

Youth Development

We are committed to nurturing the next generation of musicians through our youth program and mentorship opportunities.

Musical Innovation

While respecting tradition, we embrace new musical styles and arrangements to keep our performances fresh and engaging.
